PENDING*CONTROL

Commands

CommandArgumentsInfo
CONTINUE - Continues the pending clip playback from the current position.
FLUSH - Remove the pending clip.
PAUSE - Pauses the pending clip playback at the current position.
PLAY - Starts the pending clip playback
STOP - Stops the pending clip playback.
SWAP - Make the pending clip the active one and remove the actually active one.
SWAP_EOF - Make the pending clip the active one as soon as the active one has ended and remove the actually active one.
