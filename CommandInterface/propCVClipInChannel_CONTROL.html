CONTROL

Commands

CommandArgumentsInfo
CONTINUE - Continues the clip playback from the current position.
DEC_FRAME - Decreases the actual clip position by one frame.
FLUSH - Remove the clip(s) from the playlist.
INC_FRAME - Increases the actual clip position by one frame.
PAUSE - Pauses the clip playback at the current position.
PLAY - Starts the clip playback
STOP - Stops the clip playback.
