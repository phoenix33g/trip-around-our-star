CONTROL

Commands

CommandArgumentsInfo
CHANGE_DURATION UInt Frames
 Changed the requested number of frames to be recorded. If argument is greater than zero this amount of frames will be captured. Otherwise it keeps recording until stopped.
FLUSH - Close capture channel. The currently captured clip will be unloaded and the channel reset to its initial state.
PAUSE - Pause capturing. The capture can be resumed with CONTINUE.
RECORD UInt Frames
 Start capturing. If argument is greater than zero this amount of frames will be captured. Otherwise it keeps recording until stopped.
